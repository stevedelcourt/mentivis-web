## The mandatory mentions

Article L6353-1 frames the content of the agreement concluded between the organization and the training buyer. It specifies the title, nature, objectives and content of the action, the pedagogical and technical means, duration and dates, modalities for delivery, monitoring and assessment of results, as well as price and payment terms. These elements directly overlap with Qualiopi expectations on public information and the definition of objectives.

## Agreement, contract, purchase order

The training agreement binds the organization to a legal entity, most often a company. The training contract applies when an individual finances their own training; it contains additional protective provisions, including a right of withdrawal. A purchase order or quote does not replace the agreement: it may accompany it but does not carry the same contractual value.

## Securing edge cases

The agreement must anticipate contingencies: conditions for cancellation, postponement, withdrawal, and liability in case of non-performance. General terms and conditions cover these points. A well-constructed agreement protects the organization in case of dispute and secures coverage by funders, who require a compliant agreement to reimburse.

## Key takeaways

- The agreement follows the mandatory mentions of article L6353-1.
- It is distinguished from the individual contract, which includes a right of withdrawal.
- A purchase order does not replace the agreement.
- Cancellation, postponement and withdrawal conditions must be provided for.
- A compliant agreement conditions coverage by funders.
